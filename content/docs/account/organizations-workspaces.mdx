title: Organizations & workspaces

Hanzo Team follows a Slack-style model for structuring teams: you sign in once with your Hanzo account and can belong to many places at once.

## The model

- **Organization** — the top-level container for a company or group. Billing, plan, and org-wide membership live here.
- **Workspace** — a space inside an organization that holds projects, documents, channels, and everything else. An organization can have several workspaces (for example, one per department or product).
- **Member** — a person, identified by their Hanzo account, who belongs to one or more workspaces with a [role](/docs/advanced-settings/roles) in each.

Your single Hanzo identity spans every organization and workspace you're part of — there are no separate logins to juggle.

## Switching

Use the **account switcher** at the top of the left sidebar to move between organizations and workspaces. Switching is instant and does not sign you out — your session carries across all of them, the same way you'd switch between Slack workspaces.

![Org-grouped workspace switcher (org heading over its workspaces, Create/Invite to workspace)](/img/shell/switcher-desktop.png)

*The switcher groups workspaces under their organization. Each org heads its own list of workspaces, with **Create workspace** and **Invite to workspace** below.*

A checkmark marks the workspace you're in now. Select any other workspace to jump straight to it, or use **Create workspace** to start a new one inside the same organization.

> **On mobile:** the switcher opens as a full-width sheet with the same org-grouped list.

## The account menu

Your avatar at the bottom of the app rail opens the **account menu** — the hub for your identity, appearance, and the rest of Hanzo:

![Account menu: Profile, Theme (Light/Dark), Workspace settings, Usage & billing, Sign out](/img/shell/user-menu-desktop.png)

*The account menu: Profile, a Light/Dark theme toggle, Workspace settings, Usage & billing, the Surfaces launcher, and Sign out.*

- **Profile** — your name, avatar, and contact details.
- **Theme** — switch between Light and Dark.
- **Workspace settings** — jump into the workspace's configuration.
- **Usage & billing** — open your [wallet and plan](/docs/account/usage-billing).
- **Surfaces** — launch other Hanzo products (see [Learn the basics](/docs/getting-started/learn-the-basics#jump-to-other-hanzo-surfaces)).
- **Sign out** — end your session.

> **On mobile:** the account menu opens as a full-width sheet.

![Account menu: Profile, Theme (Light/Dark), Workspace settings, Usage & billing, Sign out (mobile)](/img/shell/user-menu-mobile.png)

*The account menu on mobile.*

## Account settings

Choose **Profile** (or the gear at the top right, then **Account settings**) to manage the parts of your Hanzo account that follow you into every workspace:

![Account settings (profile, password, integrations, notifications, calendar)](/img/settings/account-desktop.png)

*Account settings: your profile and password, plus Integrations, Notifications, Auto translation, Calendar, Office, and Mailboxes.*

These are personal to you — changing your notification preferences or connecting an integration here applies across the organizations and workspaces you belong to, not just the one you're viewing.

> **On mobile:** account settings scroll in a single column.

![Account settings (profile, password, integrations, notifications, calendar) (mobile)](/img/settings/account-mobile.png)

## Joining and leaving

- **Join** — open an invite link and sign in, or accept an invitation sent to your email. See [Workspace setup](/docs/getting-started/workspace-setup#invite-your-team).
- **Guests** — people outside your organization can be invited as guests to specific content without full membership.
- **Leave** — you can leave a workspace from the account switcher; an administrator can also remove members in **Settings → Members**.

## Administration

Workspace administrators manage members, roles, and enabled modules under **Settings**. Organization owners additionally manage the [plan and billing](/docs/account/usage-billing) that apply across the organization's workspaces.
