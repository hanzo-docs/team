This is the whole of Hanzo Team as one illustrated handbook. Read it top to bottom for a guided tour, or jump to any section. Every page below is illustrated with screenshots of the live product; the desktop view is the primary illustration, with the mobile view shown where it differs.

> **Desktop-first, for now.** Hanzo Team is built for a wide window. There's no native mobile app yet — on a phone you work through the browser, and some panes feel cramped. Each section notes what works well on mobile. See [Mobile access](/docs/getting-started/mobile-access).
