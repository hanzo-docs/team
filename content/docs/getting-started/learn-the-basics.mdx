Once you're signed in to a workspace, everything happens in the **workbench** — a single window with a global sidebar on the left and the active app on the right.

![Workbench home on the Tracker with the left app rail and project navigator](/img/shell/workbench-desktop.png)

*The workbench: the narrow app rail on the far left, the current app's navigator beside it, and the working area filling the rest.*

## The workbench

- **Account switcher** (top of the sidebar) — switch between your organizations and workspaces, manage members, and open workspace settings.
- **App sidebar** — one icon per app. Click an icon to open that app; each app has its own secondary panel for navigation.
- **Inbox** — a unified feed of notifications, mentions, and assigned work across every app.
- **Search** — jump to any issue, document, message, or contact.
- **Settings** — account preferences, workspace configuration, members, roles, and which modules are enabled.

## Action items connect the apps

The concept that ties Hanzo Team together is the **[action item](/docs/task-tracking/creating-action-items)**. When an issue is assigned to you, an action item is created automatically in your **Planner** so you can schedule it. As you work through your day, the status of the underlying issue updates itself — you rarely need to move an issue from "Todo" to "In Progress" to "Done" by hand.

> **On mobile:** the app rail and the app's own panel stack instead of sitting side by side, so you tap through one pane at a time. It's fine for quick checks; day-to-day work is smoother on a desktop.

## Jump to other Hanzo surfaces

Your Hanzo account is the same everywhere, so the account menu (your avatar, bottom-left of the rail) doubles as a launcher for the rest of Hanzo. Open it and look under **Surfaces**:

![The 5-surface launcher in the account menu: Hanzo AI, Console, App, Bot, Billing](/img/shell/app-launcher-desktop.png)

*The account menu with the five-surface launcher, plus Profile, Theme, Workspace settings, Usage & billing, and Sign out.*

- **Hanzo AI** — chat and models at [hanzo.ai](https://hanzo.ai).
- **Console** — the developer console.
- **App** — the Hanzo app.
- **Bot** — your [bots and agents](/docs/account/agents-bots).
- **Billing** — plans, wallet, and invoices at [billing.hanzo.ai](https://billing.hanzo.ai).

The same menu holds **Profile**, a **Theme** toggle (Light / Dark), **Workspace settings**, **Usage & billing**, and **Sign out**.

> **On mobile:** the account menu opens as a full-width sheet, so every surface stays one tap away.

![The 5-surface launcher in the account menu: Hanzo AI, Console, App, Bot, Billing (mobile)](/img/shell/app-launcher-mobile.png)

*The launcher as a full-width sheet on mobile.*
